Our Food

Cose Buone cooks up romantic, rustic Italian food from the heart of Sicily – a region that boasts fantastic dishes like arancine and cannoli. Cannoli are delicate little rolls of crispy pastry packed with sweet ricotta – try one!

Who Are We?

We are Alex & Mauro, the Managing Directors of Cose Buone Liverpool. We moved to Liverpool in 2017 to make our dream a reality. We are both originally from Palermo in Sicily and enjoy creating homemade delicacies for you all to try!
